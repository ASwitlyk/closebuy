#closebuy#

## Sub-Heading ##
  Buy & Sell things locally in seconds!!

## Summary ##

We think that selling an item should be as easy as taking a picture, and discovering new purchases as easy as swiping a finger. 

Closebuy links buyers & sellers in the local marketplace creating a simple & fun shopping experience.

## Problem ##
  Selling products is a tedious process involving filling up long forms and thus makes people reluctant.

## Solution ##
  Closebuy eliminates the hassles of selling products in the current marketplace by creating an efficient and quickest way of making your items available.
